Who are we?

We are a charitable organisation dedicated to fighting for underpriveleged peoples across the globe, based in Dublin, Ireland our mission statement is clear, solve the economic injustice and inequality found in the world today,We provide shelter,food and clothing to those in need and will not stop until the issues are solved.

what are the sustainable development goals?

The sustainable development goals were a set of goals agreed upon by all un member states in 2015, these goals were a mix of objectives to help solve the current inequaloty, financial and enviromental crises facing the world currently. These goals are aimed to be hit by 2030,
