No Poverty Targets

Mouse over the squares!

  • By 2030, eradicate extreme poverty for all people everywhere,(currently measured as living on less than $2.15 a day).
  • By 2030, reduce at least by half the proportions of people of all ages living in poverty in all its dimensions according to national definitions'
  • Implement nationally appropiate social protection systems and measures for all, including floors, and by 2030 achieve substantial coverage of the poor and the vulnerable.
  • By 2030, ensure everyone,(in particular the poor and vulnerable), have equal rights to economic resources, aswell as basic services, ownership and control over all forms of property, inheritance and natural resources.
  • By 2030, build the resilience of the poor and vulnerable and reduce their exposure to climate related extreme events and other social, economic and enviromental shocks and disasters.
